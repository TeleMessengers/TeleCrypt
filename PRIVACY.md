## 2. Data Processed

Tammy processes and caches data locally on your device. This data includes:

- Matrix User ID
- Access token for Matrix Homeserver usage
- End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) keys
- Chat history
- Metadata required for the proper functioning of the app

This data remains stored locally and is not processed or transmitted outside of the app.

### **Note on Matrix Homeserver**

Please note that Tammy communicates with a Matrix Homeserver for the purpose of exchanging
messages and related metadata. The processing of data by the Matrix Homeserver is outside the scope of this privacy
notice. The privacy policy of the Homeserver operator governs the data processed and stored by the Homeserver. Please
consult the privacy notice of your chosen Homeserver for more information.

## 3. Purpose of Processing

The data is collected and cached solely for the purpose of providing you with messaging services through the Matrix
protocol. All data processing occurs within the app on your device and is necessary for its functionality (e.g.,
ensuring secure communications through encryption and storing chat history for access).

## 4. Data Retention

The data processed by the app is stored exclusively on your device. There is no external storage or retention of
personal data. You have full control over the data, and it will be deleted if you uninstall the app.

## 5. Security Measures

We implement the standard Matrix End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) in encrypted rooms to ensure that your communications are
protected. Please note that not all rooms are encrypted, and encryption is applied only where enabled by the user or
server administrator.
