# Privacy Policy for TeleCrypt Messenger

**Effective Date:** January 2025

## Introduction

TeleCrypt is a secure messaging application built on the Matrix protocol. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

## Data We Process

### Local Data Storage
When you use TeleCrypt, the following data is processed and stored locally on your device:

- Matrix User ID
- Access tokens for Matrix homeserver connection
- End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) keys
- Chat history and messages
- Metadata necessary for app functionality

### Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. All data processing occurs locally on your device and is essential for the app's functionality.

## Data Security

- **End-to-End Encryption**: We use E2EE by default to protect your communications
- **Local Storage**: All data remains on your device
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store your messages or personal data on our servers

## Third-Party Services

TeleCrypt may connect to Matrix homeservers (which you choose) to send and receive messages. The privacy practices of these homeservers are governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Camera and Microphone

TeleCrypt may request camera and microphone permissions for:
- Video and voice calls
- Photo and video sharing
- QR code scanning

These permissions are used only for the features you actively use and do not result in data collection by us.

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app or create an issue in our GitHub repository.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

---

*This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to TeleCrypt Messenger.*
